Plantzr mobile redesign

A face-lift for the Plantzr mobile application.

The screens have been wireframed and redesigned in Sketch, and then prototyped in Framer.

Search results are displayed using cards. Icons indicate the plant type, as well as main categories: Edible, Nitrogen fixer and Ornemental.

Search screen prototype

The search form is now integrated in the home screen, and opens with a drop down animation.

The form opens on click, and is swiped up to be hidden.
