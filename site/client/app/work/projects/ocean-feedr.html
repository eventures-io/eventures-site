Ocean feedr

Ocean Feedr is an rss feed aggregator of ocean environmental related articles.

The user interface is an AngularJS version of Codropsâ€™ grid animation layout.

Feeds are loaded and parsed on the backend, and are available to the frontend through pagination for infinite scrolling.

Ocean feedr also keeps track of ongoing environmental campaigns relating to ocean health preservation. It allows visitors to take immediate action on a number of issues that need public support.
