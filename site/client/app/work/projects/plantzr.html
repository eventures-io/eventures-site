Plantzr

Coming from the northern hemisphere where I was familiar with the very common plants like Hogweed, Dandelion and trees like Oak, Beech and Birch, most plants encoutered here in the south of Europe where unknown to me.

Carob tree, Rubber fig, Aloe and Agave varieties, Oleander, Giant cane are among those very common plants that had no name to me.

So I set out to make an inventory of plants I didn’t know, so I could find their names and properties later on.

Plantzr is a web application, based on the MEAN-stack, that allows to create (and edit) a descriptive card for each plant species I want to document. To be able to upload photo’s directly from the field, I also added a native mobile application. See plantzr-mobile
