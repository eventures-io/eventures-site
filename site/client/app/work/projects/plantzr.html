Plantzr

Recently I moved from Brussels to Lisbon. Coming from the northern hemisphere where I was familiar with the very common plants like Hogweed, Dandelion and trees like Oak, Beech and Birch, most plants encoutered here in the south of Europe where unknown to me.

Carob tree, Rubber fig, Aloe and Agave varieties, Oleander, Giant cane are among those very common plants that I had no name for.

So I set out to make an inventory of plants I didn’t know, and document them progressively.

Plantzr is a web application, based on the MEAN-stack, that allows to create (and edit) a descriptive card for each plant species I want to document. To be able to upload photo’s directly from the field, I also added a native mobile application.
